Choclate Cake recipe

This is a very simple and delicious recipe for a choclate cake.

Ingridients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup boiling water

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees C
  2. Stir sugar, flour, baking powder and salt together in a large bowl.
  3. Add eggs and milk mix it with an mixer.
  4. Stir in the boiling water. The batter will be thin.
  5. Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
  6. Bake it in over for 35 minutes. once finished cool in the pans for 10 mins
  7. And last but not least your Choclate Cake is ready. enjoy!
